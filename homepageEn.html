Welcome to Amax Academy of Oakville (AAO)!

Language is a tool for communication, the direct reality of thoughts, the carrier and container of culture, and also the basis of learning other subjects. A person’s linguistic abilities affect all aspects of his/her life, be it academic, professional or even within family.

Amax Academy of Oakville (AAO) is a school focusing on Chinese and English language education. In our opinion, a language is an active system, a complex "habit" system. The formation of a habit needs a lot of practice. In language learning, from "input" to "output", from "comprehension" to "composition", a lot of systematic practice is the key to turn the language materials learned into real linguistic competence.


Teaching features

  • Emphasis on “language output”, i.e. writing & speaking abilities
  • Students being the main players of the class & teacher as a guide
  • Very small class (4-6 students)

Missions

  • To bridge cultures through language teaching and build a bilingual community
  • To love students, be strict with them and nurture excellence
  • To develop students’ thinking abilities and tap their potential through solid practice
